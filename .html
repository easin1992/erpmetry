Overview

We are creating the ERPmetry application user manual. ERPmetry is a comprehensive, fully functional ERP solution designed and built by our company. It is tailored for the spinning mill industry in Bangladesh, where such systems are still uncommon. Our company built it end‑to‑end, and users are happy to have a platform like this supporting daily operations.

Note: This document is an all‑in‑one HTML manual you can print to PDF or split into separate pages later.

Why it’s unique for spinning mills

  • Industry‑specific workflows: bale to yarn, lot tracking, waste handling.
  • Bangladesh localization: documents, units, taxes, and reports familiar to local teams.
  • Real‑time dashboards and KPIs across production, stock, and sales.
  • Offline‑tolerant data entry patterns for unstable connectivity.

System requirements

  • Modern browser (Chrome, Edge, Firefox, Safari) updated in the last 12 months.
  • Recommended screen ≥ 1366×768, best at 1600×900 or more.
  • Stable network connection; VPN if required by your company.
  • Authorized user account with assigned role.

Access & Login

  1. Open the ERPmetry URL provided by IT.
  2. Enter your Username and Password.
  3. Click Sign In. If you forget your password, use Forgot Password or contact IT.

Tip: Use Ctrl + K to open quick search (if enabled).

Global navigation

  • Top Bar: breadcrumbs, profile, notifications.
  • Side Menu: modules and sub‑menus.
  • Footer: version and environment label (Test/Prod).

HR Module

Employee Information

Create new employee → fill personal & job info → save. Use search to edit existing employees.

Worker Scheduling

Define off‑day schedules and deductions. Export to CSV for audits.

Production Module

Lot & Shift Entry

Record shift output, waste, and quality metrics. Use batch numbers for traceability.

KPI Cards

Monitor total output, efficiency, and downtime trends.

Inventory Module

Goods Receipt (GRN)

Scan or select PO → verify quantities → post to stock.

Stock Ledger

Real‑time in/out with filters by item, lot, and warehouse.

Sales Module

Delivery Orders & Challans

Create DOs, print challans, track delivered vs pending quantities.

Invoices

Generate tax‑compliant invoices with audit trail.

Finance Module

  • Chart of Accounts, vouchers, payments, receipts.
  • Integration with inventory and sales for COGS and revenue.

Reports & Analytics

Standard reports (production, stock, sales), plus custom dashboards.

Settings

  • Company profile, units, tax rules, numbering formats.
  • Document templates (print & PDF).

Users & Roles

Create roles → assign permissions → add users → map to roles. Use 2FA if enabled.

Audit & Logs

Every critical change is tracked with who/when/what. Use filters to review revisions.

FAQ

Q: I can’t login? A: Check caps lock, reset password, or contact IT.

Q: Where are my reports? A: Go to Reports & Analytics from the side menu.
