Overview

ERPmetry is a full-featured ERP system built by our company for spinning mills in Bangladesh. It supports production, inventory, sales, finance and HR workflows. This manual explains how to use the system and how developers can extend it.

Note: This plain HTML manual is intended for easy reading and editing by new developers.

Setup & Access

  1. Open the ERPmetry web URL given by your IT team.
  2. Sign in with your username and password.
  3. If you forget your password, use the "Forgot Password" link or contact support.

Shortcut tip: Press Ctrl + K for quick search (if enabled).

Modules

The main modules in ERPmetry are:

  • HR — Employee records, schedules, payroll basics.
  • Production — Lot/shift entry, machine output, waste tracking.
  • Inventory — Goods receipt (GRN), stock ledger, lot tracking.
  • Sales — Orders, delivery orders (DO), invoices, challans.
  • Finance — Chart of accounts, vouchers, payments, reports.

Administration

Common admin tasks:

  • Create user accounts and assign roles.
  • Configure company settings: units, tax rates, print templates.
  • View audit logs to see who changed records and when.

FAQ

Q: I cannot log in.
A: Check that Caps Lock is off, confirm your username, and try resetting your password.

Q: Where are reports?
A: Go to the Reports section from the main menu and select the report category you need.
